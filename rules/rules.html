Platform Rules & Regulations

Review our code of conduct, modification compliance, and formal tier qualification requirements.

About Shift PvP

Shift PvP operates as an independent, premier standard for competitive rank mapping inside the contemporary Minecraft ecosystem. Our vision is to secure a completely balance-oriented, competitive ecosystem devoid of external unfair mechanics. By evaluating metrics over an array of custom-tailored environments, Shift PvP accurately validates mechanics, strategies, and physical execution across multiple distinct competitive modes. Transparency and strict adherence to game integrity remain the foundation of our leaderboard rankings.

Shift PvP Tier Testing Rules

01

No Cheating

The use of hacked clients, unfair modifications, exploits, or any software that provides an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. If you are unsure whether a mod or client is allowed, open a support ticket before using it.

02

Keep Chat Respectful

Swearing, offensive language, harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior is not allowed in-game or on Discord. Keep the community welcoming and respectful for everyone.

03

No NSFW Content

Any NSFW content, including messages, images, skins, usernames, or other inappropriate content, is strictly prohibited.

04

Litematica Usage

Litematica is allowed for building purposes only. Features such as Easy Place and Air Place may only be used while constructing builds.

05

Auto Clickers

Auto clickers may only be used for farming or other approved AFK activities. Using an auto clicker during PvP or PvP tier testing is strictly forbidden and may result in punishment.

06

Alternate Accounts (Alts)

Players may use up to three alternate accounts after receiving approval through a Discord support ticket. Using unapproved alts may result in punishment.

07

No Scamming

Scamming, misleading, or attempting to steal items, currency, or accounts from other players is strictly prohibited.

08

No Combat Logging

Do not disconnect or intentionally leave the server to avoid dying or losing a fight during PvP.

Tier Test Requirement

You must invite at least 2 members to the Shift PvP Discord server before requesting a Tier Test.
